Fatal Influence

Psychological Thriller Feature / Series Concept

A slow-burn psychological thriller dissecting modern social media validation, curated boundaries, and toxic intimacy. The story traces the unsettling collision between Jada, an over-leveraged micro-influencer fighting legal ruin, and Sasha, an analytical predator wearing a synthetic fitness lifestyle like armor.

THE CORE CONFLICT

"What happens when your most intimate, hidden wounds are downloaded, calculated, and turned against you as marketing software?"

TREATMENT SEGMENT // ACT II CALIBRATION REVISED
THE SCENE PROFILE

At a coffee shop corner, Sasha treats a consulting session like a masterclass, absorbing Jada's lighting parameters, editing speed-ramp workflows, and visual engagement philosophies while recording notes.

THE CORE CONVERSATION / BOUNDARY

When Sasha touches her hands, Jada pulls back, opening up about a semi-abusive childhood and sudden touch aversions. Sasha comforts her with deeply validating empathy, absorbing the psychological blueprints of Jada's trust.

THE INTERCEPTION

Hayden arrives, reaching for Jada's shoulder. Tracking his hand, Sasha immediately blocks the physical contact with a sudden, off-topic, high-energy compliment on his clothes. The vocal block forces Hayden to drop his hand. This leaves Hayden suspicious of her synthetic vibe, but prompts Jada to snap at him, fiercely defending Sasha as the only one truly respecting her space.
